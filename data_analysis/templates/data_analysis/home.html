View Year wise Cut-off

Our "View Year-wise Cut-off" feature is designed to help prospective students and their families gain insights into the admission trends for various engineering colleges participating in the Joint Seat Allocation Authority (JoSAA) process. This tool provides a comprehensive and user-friendly way to access historical cut-off ranks for different institutions, branches, and categories.

Analyse Gender wise cut-off trends

Our "Analyse Gender-wise Cut-off Trends" feature is a powerful tool designed to provide deep insights into the admission patterns based on gender across various engineering colleges participating in the Joint Seat Allocation Authority (JoSAA) process. This feature helps prospective students understand how gender-specific cut-off ranks have evolved over the years, offering valuable information for making informed decisions.

Round wise cut-off Analysis

Our "Round-wise Cut-off Analysis" feature provides an in-depth look at the admission cut-off ranks for each round of the Joint Seat Allocation Authority (JoSAA) process. This tool is designed to help prospective students understand how the cut-off ranks evolve through different rounds of seat allocation, offering critical insights for strategic planning during the admission process.
